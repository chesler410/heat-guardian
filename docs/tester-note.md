# Heat Guardian — note for test users (pilot at the meet)

*Paste this into the TestFlight/Play tester invite, a team chat, or hand it out at the meet.*

---

**Thanks for trying Heat Guardian this weekend! 🦈 This is an early test build — here's the quick version.**

**What it does:** add the meet's heat sheet, tap your swimmer(s), and see all their events, heats, lanes, the next time cut to chase, and fueling tips — on one screen. After a race, log the time they swam. There's also optional AI "how'd it go" feedback after the meet.

**One import can cover the whole team.** If one of us imports the meet and shares the link/code, the rest of us can just **join** it instead of importing our own copy.

**What leaves your device (so you know):**
- By default, everything stays **on your phone** — your swimmers and meet data live only in the app.
- If you **share a meet** by link or code, that meet's sheet is uploaded so others can join. It **auto-expires after the meet.**
- If you use **AI feedback**, it sends the swim times you logged — **never your swimmer's name.**
- No ads, nothing sold, no account/login.

**Please double-check times and heats** against the official posted results — the app reads PDFs automatically and isn't perfect, and any time someone enters by hand can have a typo. The **live/official** result is always the real one.

**Hit a bug or have an idea?** Use the in-app **Send feedback** link (or your TestFlight/Play screenshot feedback). Tell me what you tapped and what happened — rough is fine. That's exactly what this weekend is for.
