# Heat Guardian — Store Listing Kit (App Store + Google Play)

Ready-to-paste metadata + ASO (App Store Optimization) guidance. **Trademark-safe:** never put
"USA Swimming" or "Meet Mobile" in any store field (name/subtitle/keywords/description) — implies
affiliation and risks rejection. Describe functionally instead. Category: **Sports**.

---

## How discovery actually works (do this well)

- **App Store search** ranks mainly on: **app name**, **subtitle**, and the **100-char keyword field**
  (NOT the description). So every character of those three is precious — no wasted/duplicate words.
- **Google Play search** indexes the **title**, **short description**, AND the **full description** —
  so weave key terms naturally into the long description (2–3x, not stuffed).
- **Ratings & reviews** drive ranking on both. The in-app ⭐ rate prompt is already built (turns on
  once the store URLs are set) — lean on it.
- **Localized listings rank in each locale's store.** Localizing at least the title/subtitle/short
  description in the 8 supported languages widens reach a lot (full descriptions can follow).
- **Screenshots** don't affect search rank but drive install *conversion* — the first 2 matter most.

## Shared full description (English)

Heat Guardian is the calm, ad-free companion for swim meet day — built for the parents, swimmers, and coaches who want the whole meet in their pocket.

Import a meet's heat sheet (upload the PDF or paste a public link) and instantly see every one of your swimmers' events on one clean page — event, heat, lane, and seed time — organized by day and session. No more flipping through pages on the pool deck.

FEATURES
• Heat sheet import — upload a PDF or paste a link; your swimmers' events are pulled out automatically.
• Meet-day home — everyone's races by day and session, with heat, lane, and up-next order.
• Time standards & cuts — the motivational grade for each event and exactly how much to drop for the next cut and championship qualifying times.
• Splits & pacing — plan goal splits and time each swim on deck with the live split timer.
• Live results — paste a meet's live-results link and watch times overlay as they post.
• Swimmer profiles — look up any competitive swimmer for their best times, motivational standards, and recent meet results; follow your own kids and the ones to chase.
• Find a meet — search meets by name or region.
• Private by design — your swimmers and data stay on your device. No accounts. No ads. Ever.

Free to use. If Heat Guardian helps on meet day, an optional tip keeps it going.

Not affiliated with any swim governing body, timing company, or results app. Always verify event, heat, and lane against the official posted heat sheet before a race.
