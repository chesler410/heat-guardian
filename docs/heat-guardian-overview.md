# 🏊 Heat Guardian — a calm meet-day companion for swim parents

**Heat Guardian** turns the chaos of a swim meet into a calm, glanceable plan. Import the heat
sheet or results once, and it shows you — at a glance — what your swimmer is swimming, when, and
how close they are to their next time cut. Built for stressed parents on a loud pool deck.

*Free to try. No account. Works on your phone (iOS + Android) and the web.*

---

## What it does

- **⏱ Up Next** — the soonest races for your swimmer(s), with heat + lane, right at the top.
- **The arm table** — track *multiple* swimmers across the day at a glance (the classic "which
  heat, which lane, what time" board), with day-by-day grouping that auto-focuses on today.
- **Time cuts that mean something** — see exactly how many seconds your swimmer needs for their
  next cut (A/AA/AAA…) and their championship qualifying time, per event.
- **Fueling & warm-up tips** — gentle meet-day reminders.
- **🔴 Live results** — paste a meet's real-time results link and actual times light up as they
  swim (no more refreshing a clunky site).
- **📍 Find a meet near you** — uses your location to surface upcoming meets nearby.
- **🤝 Share a meet by code** — one person imports the big heat sheet once; the whole team pulls
  it with a short code (no re-downloading huge PDFs on every phone).
- **🏊 Swimmer mode ("My Meet")** — for a kid managing their own meet: see your events, highlight
  friends, jot a reflection on each swim, and get **✨ encouraging AI feedback** on how it went.
- **8 languages**, light/dark, and your team's logo + colors in the header.

## Private by design

Everything lives **on your device** — no account, no sign-up, nothing tracked. It's built to be
safe for kids (COPPA-minded): location is only used to find nearby meets and never leaves your
phone; the optional AI feedback sees only swim times and your child's own notes — never their name.
